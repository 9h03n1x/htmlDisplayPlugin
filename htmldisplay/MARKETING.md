# Info Display for Stream Deck

**Instantly display rich HTML and Markdown content with a single button press.**

---

## Overview

Info Display is a powerful Stream Deck plugin that lets you show custom HTML or Markdown content in a sleek popup window or your default browser. Perfect for streamers, content creators, developers, and professionals who need quick access to formatted information.

## How It Works

1. **Add an Action** ‚Äì Drag "Show HTML" or "Show Markdown" to your Stream Deck
2. **Configure Content** ‚Äì Enter your content directly or select a file
3. **Customize Display** ‚Äì Choose theme, window mode, and size
4. **Press & Display** ‚Äì Hit the button to show your content instantly
